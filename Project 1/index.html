ABOUT US

Vekan Martial Arts Sports Academy

Our journey began at the Government Industrial Training Institute in Tiruvannamalai since 2013, supported by the government's provision of land for practice. From these humble origins, our classes have evolved into an Indoor Academy proudly known as Vekan Martial Arts Sports Academy. It is with great pride that we declare ourselves as the first indoor class in Tiruvannamalai district. Our commitment to excellence and innovation continues to shape the martial arts experience we offer. Join us as we celebrate the rich history and progress of Vekan Martial Arts Sports Academy.
